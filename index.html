What do your customers do if the time they want isn't available?

Bump lets you capture all those customers that try to book a slot at your business but are unable to find their preferred time.

Bump will notify those customers when their preferred time slot opens up.

Never turn away a customer again.

Let us ask you a question...

A customer comes to your site to book your service.
You are booked solid at their preferred time.
What options do they have?

They could go elsewhere?
You risk losing this customer forever. No good.
They could call your business and request a callback if the time opens up!
This is a lot of admin. No good.
You could email them? Or call them?
People generally ignore emails, and if they miss your call - no good.
They could book a less convenient time!
This increases the chance of a late show or no show!

But what if they could have something listen for cancellations for them?

That's Bump!

Bump does 3 things, very, very well.

  • Easy to implement waitlist
  • Auto fill cancellations for you with interested customers
  • Demand and Pricing Analytics

Very easy. Very useful.
